# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard designed to analyze sales performance across different regions, product categories, sales representatives, and cities. The dashboard provides valuable insights that support data-driven business decisions.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze sales performance across different regions.
- Identify top-performing sales representatives.
- Determine the most profitable products.
- Monitor customer growth trends.
- Compare Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) across cities.
- Provide actionable business insights through data visualization.

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Power BI
- Data Visualization
- Business Analytics

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains sales transaction records, including:

- Sales Revenue
- Profit
- Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
- Customer Information
- Product Categories
- Sales Representatives
- Regions
- Cities

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- 💰 Total Revenue KPI
- 📊 Total Profit KPI
- 🏙️ Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) by City
- 👥 Total Customers
- 🏆 Top Performing Sales Representatives
- 📦 Most Profitable Products
- 📅 Monthly Customer Count Trend
- 🌍 Regional Filters
- 🛍️ Product Category Filters

## 🔍 Key Insights

- The South region recorded the strongest overall sales performance.
- Laptops, desktops, and sofa products generated the highest revenue and profit.
- Electronics and furniture delivered the highest profitability.
- March recorded the highest customer activity.
- January recorded the lowest customer activity.
- Lagos, Kano, Abuja, and Port Harcourt contributed significantly to overall sales.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-performing products.
- Reward top-performing sales representatives.
- Expand marketing efforts in high-performing regions.
- Improve sales strategies during low-performing months.
- Focus on customer retention to drive repeat purchases.
